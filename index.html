mutant meeples

How to play

The aim of mutant meeples is to move 6 individual meeples onto the target. Select the meeples with the coloured buttons below or the individual number keys.

Meeples have SUPER SPEED. When you click an ARROW KEY they move as far as possible in that direction and stop only when they hit a wall, another meeple or the edge of the board.

On each run to the target you can only use THREE meeples. Each meeple has a special ability accessed with the star buttons.
  • GREEN - jump over two spaces in any direction
  • GREY - move THROUGH the first wall in any direction
  • BLUE - jump to any of the blue warp spots.
  • BROWN - STOP one space short of a wall or meeple
  • RED - step ONE space up, down left or right
  • WHITE - step ONE space diagonally
  • YELLOW - WRAP around the edge of the board
  • BLACK - use the ability of ANY other meeple as long as that meeple hasn't yet used their ability
